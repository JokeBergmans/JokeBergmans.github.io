DISC-test

The DISC-test is supposed to give participants an insight into their own personality style, behaviour and way of communicating. To achieve this, behavioral characteristics are grouped in 4 styles: Dominant, Influence, Stable and Conscientious.

My most dominant behavioral style is Conscientious, followed by Stable. With these results it is now possible to make an estimate of my strengths and weaknesses:

Strengths

  • Usually satisfied and can adapt herself
  • Not very combative
  • More careful than impulsive, avoids risks
  • Acts in a tactful, diplomatic way and strives for a stable and orderly life
  • Is fine with working and living according to established procedures
  • Patient, calm and self-possessed
  • Very willing to help others - especially people who she regards as friends
  • Analytical

Weaknesses

  • Rather lets others take the initiative or solve problems first
  • Is less socially active
  • Is less open to challenges
  • Has more problems dealing with unexpected situations

I can certainly agree with these results, both at school and in my daily life I am a rather quiet person and I attach great importance to order and routine. The weak points are work points that I am aware of, especially the trouble to take initiative is very recognizable. In a team I therefore need a leader who can assign tasks. I also work easier towards deadlines.
I would not say that this test has taught me a lot, but it is certainly enriching to be able to list strengths and weaknesses in order to gain more insight into my personality. This test can also help with collaboration with others, such as the team passport that we had to compile for InnovIT.

Enneagram test

Het Enneagram is een persoonlijkheidsmodel dat zich met bewust of onbewust gedrag bezighoudt. Het beschrijft 9 persoonlijkheidstypes, die de motivatie aan vanwaaruit in veel omstandigheden gehandeld of gedacht wordt geven. Volgens het Enneagram is het ene type niet beter dan het andere. Elk type heeft eigenschappen die doeltreffend kunnen zijn.
Types worden toegekend door het verdelen van 100 punten. Het type met het meeste punten sluit het dichtst aan bij de persoonlijkheid.
Daarnaast is er ook sprake van types-verhoudingen: als iemand ontspannen is, neemt hij de positieve eigenschappen van een ander type over, en in gespannen situaties de negatieve eigenschappen van een ander type.



Perfectionist - 17 points

  • Realistic
  • Conscientious
  • Thorough
  • Firm

Receives appreciation because he knows how things are supposed to be. He also knows exactly how to perfectly execute everything. He has a sense of humor and is goal and solution oriented. He likes rules and structure and constantly strives for the very best. And of course he can do everything better. He not only sets high standards for himself, but also for others. He is annoyed by mistakes and has a great sense of responsibility.

Romantic - 17 points

  • Sensitive
  • Warm
  • Shrewd

Is sensitive, creative, emotionally intense and reserved. He receives appreciation from others because he is so special and he gives the world color. He is able to look inside, is a romantic type. He has great empathy. He is often focused on the inner adventure.

I gave equal points to both types, and this really matches my personality. I am very fond of order and structure, but I also have a creative and sensitive side. To outsiders I am rather reserved, making the characteristics of the Perfectionist become more obvious.
When I look at the type relationships, I can mainly agree with the Perfectionist: negative characteristics of the Romantic in stressful circumstances (envious, dramatizing, focus on what is missing and not on what is present) and positive of Epicure in relaxed situations (energetic, lively and optimistic). In this area, my personality is more in line with the Perfectionist. Wanneer ik de types-verhoudingen bekijk, kan ik me vooral vinden in de Perfectionist: negatieve eigenschappen van de Romanticus in stressvolle omstandigheden (afgunstig, dramatiseren, focus op wat mist en niet op wat er wel is) en positieve van de Avonturier in ontspannen situaties (energiek, levendig en optimistisch). Op dit vlak sluit mijn persoonlijkheid meer aan bij de Perfectionist.

InnovIT

The purpose of this assignment was to come up with an innovative application related to one of 4 social themes (environment, safety, mobility and health). We expanded this concept further and further, resulting in mock-ups and a commercial that we used to pitch our idea at the InnovIT Awards.

Our idea

bEETer is an app made for people with a heart for mother nature who want to help the environment by purchasing local food. On one hand you can search for a product to receive a list of available traders who offer this product. These can be shops, but also local farms that grow and sell their own fruit and vegetables. On the other hand, the app can scan product barcodes and trace their origin. The full travel route of the product is shown to be able to grasp how far some products are transported. In addition, a local variant is recommended, with the nearest location where this can be obtained.

Our goal was to show that small changes in our buying behavior can have a major impact on the environment. The amount of CO2 that is emitted during transport is enormous, and also unnecessary when you see how many local alternatives can be found. We hope to find a smaller range of international products in the future, so that people are forced to live more environmentally conscious.
